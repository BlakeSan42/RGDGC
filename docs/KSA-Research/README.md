## Why This Matters to RGDGC

River Grove Park is a **private park** owned by KSA. Our disc golf club operates on KSA land, which means:

- All players must be Kingwood residents (KSA member village) or find alternative parking
- KSA controls park access, maintenance, and improvements
- Any course expansion or infrastructure (signage, tee pads, baskets) requires KSA approval
- Understanding KSA's financial health and governance helps us plan long-term

## Key Facts

- **KSA Founded:** September 13, 1976
- **Tax Status:** 501(c)(4) civic league (EIN: 74-1891991)
- **Annual Revenue:** ~$1M (stable since 2011)
- **Total Assets:** ~$3.1M (2024)
- **Parks Owned:** 5 parks, 356.6 acres total
- **River Grove Park:** 74 acres, privately owned by KSA
- **Assessment Fee:** $295/lot/year (2025)
- **Employees:** 0 (all-volunteer board, outsourced management)
